Who We Are

Hammad Adam Group (HAG) has been a trailblazer in the sports industry since its inception in 1994, consistently setting new benchmarks of excellence. As a market hype creator, HAG has propelled itself to remarkable heights, earning a stellar reputation for delivering premium-quality sportswear, equipment, and a diverse range of related products. With an unwavering commitment to innovation, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction, the company has gained the trust of clients worldwide, solidifying its position as a global leader in the industry.

Shakeel Chohan is a visionary entrepreneur and the esteemed Chairman of Hammad Adam Group (HAG). With a distinguished career spanning over three decades, he has been at the forefront of the sportswear and sports equipment industry since 1994. Renowned for his unwavering commitment to excellence, he has cultivated a legacy built on trust, integrity, and superior craftsmanship. His keen business acumen and relentless pursuit of quality have positioned HAG as a leading name in the global market, ensuring that every product reflects the highest standards of innovation, durability, and precision. Through his leadership, HAG continues to thrive, setting benchmarks in the industry and earning the confidence of customers worldwide.

Hammad Chohan is a dynamic and forward-thinking entrepreneur with a passion for revolutionizing the sports industry through innovative and strategic ideas. As the Managing Director of Hammad Adam Group (HAG) since 2018, he has been instrumental in driving the company toward new horizons of success. With a keen eye for market trends and a commitment to excellence, he continuously seeks to modernize and elevate the business, introducing cutting-edge solutions that redefine industry standards. His leadership embodies ambition, creativity, and determination, ensuring that HAG remains at the forefront of the global sports market.

OUR MISSION

At HammadAdam Group, our mission is to ensure customer satisfaction by delivering high-quality sportswear and equipment that meet the highest standards of performance and durability. We are committed to innovation, craftsmanship, and excellence, striving to provide athletes and sports enthusiasts with products that enhance their experience. With a focus on reliability and customer trust, HammadAdam Group continues to set benchmarks in the industry, offering premium solutions tailored to the needs of our valued clients.

FACTORY UNIT Tour

TRACK SUIT UNIT

This unit is responsible for the production of high-performance tracksuits, incorporating advanced textile engineering for optimal comfort and mobility. The manufacturing process includes fabric cutting, seam reinforcement, elastic waistband integration, and zipper attachment, ensuring a tailored fit and durability. The unit focuses on designing modern, lightweight, and breathable sportswear suitable for training and casual wear. Let me know if you need further refinements!

BOXING UNIT

This unit specializes in the precision crafting of high-quality boxing gloves. Utilizing durable synthetic and genuine leather materials, the production process involves cutting, stitching, padding insertion, and reinforced seam construction to ensure maximum durability and hand protection. Each glove undergoes rigorous quality control to maintain ergonomic fit, comfort, and impact resistance, catering to professional and amateur athletes alike.

JIU-JITSU SUIT UNIT

The Jiu-Jitsu Suit Unit is dedicated to the meticulous fabrication of traditional and competition-grade gi uniforms. The process includes fabric selection, reinforced stitching for stress points, precise panel assembly, and embroidery detailing. Special attention is given to durability and flexibility, ensuring that the final product withstands intense grappling movements while providing comfort and breathability.
